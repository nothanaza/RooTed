About RooTed DBN

We are a Christian youth and teen development program dedicated to nurturing young believers in their faith journey

Our Mission

RooTed DBN exists to empower teens and youth to develop a deep, authentic relationship with Jesus Christ while equipping them with the tools they need to navigate life's challenges with faith and confidence.

Through dynamic programs, engaging activities, and a supportive community, we create an environment where young believers can grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, and discover their purpose in God's kingdom.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of rooted, resilient young Christians who are passionate about their faith, equipped to make a difference in their communities, and committed to living out God's purpose for their lives.

We envision a community where every teen and young person feels valued, supported, and empowered to become the leaders God has called them to be.
